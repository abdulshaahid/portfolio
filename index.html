About

Known for his exceptional performances in Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi, Dulquer Salmaan is one of the best and most versatile actors in the Indian film industry. After his debut in 2012, Dulquer has carved out a unique niche for himself as an actor, singer, dancer, and producer. In 2016, he was awarded the Kerala State Award for Best Actor for his performance in Charlie. His 2021 release Kurup was the first Indian film to have an NFT collectible and became one of the highest-grossing Malayalam movies of all times. In 2022, he had four separate releases- one each in Tamil, Hindi, Malayalam, and Telugu- a very rare and almost unmatchable feat for an Indian actor. His 2022 release Sita Ramam was a major commercial success globally, collecting Rs. 95 crores at the box office. For his Hindi movie Chup, he won the Dadasaheb Phalke International Film Festival Awards 2023 for best actor in a negative role. The actor also bagged the BBC Top Gear India Award 2023 for Petrolhead Actor of the Year. The same year, his Netflix series Guns & Gulaab, in which he plays the lead alongside Rajkumar Rao, was critically acclaimed and has a 7.7 rating on imdb. The proof of Dulquer’s burgeoning popularity is in the opportunities that come his way. In 2019, he became the first ever actor from Kerala to be featured on the cover of Vogue India. In 2023, he was featured on the cover of BBC Top Gear magazine becoming just the second Indian actor and first South Indian actor to do so. A keen auto enthusiast, Dulquer Salmaan is also an influential presence on social media, amassing almost 14.3 million followers on Instagram. He also has more than 6 million followers on Facebook and more than 2.5 million followers on Twitter. The son of legendary Malayalam actor Mammootty, Dulquer is also an entrepreneur, with a special interest in clean energy. One of the companies he has invested in, Ultraviolette Automotive recently launched its first e-bike. In 2019, he established his own production company Wayfarer Films, which has supported several newcomers in the industry.
